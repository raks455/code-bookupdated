About BIM

The Tribhuvan University Faculty of Management offers a 4-year undergraduate degree called the Bachelor in Information Management (BIM). It is a combined information technology and management course. Students can learn information technology abilities while also understanding professional competence in policy making thanks to the manner that the BIM course structure is created. The study of information technology and communication systems in business and administration is known as information management. The 126 credit hour BIM program uses a semester schedule for delivery, and evaluation is done using a grading system. More than 26 Business/IT institutes in Nepal offer the Bachelor of Information Management (BIM) program with affiliation to Tribhuvan University.

About Bsc.CSIT

The four-year Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc. CSIT) program at Tribhuvan University is intended to give students a comprehensive education in computing and information technology. Currently, Nepal has 57 colleges that offer the B.Sc. CSIT program. Along with the usual lectures, the BSc CSIT program includes a substantial amount of practical and project work. The program covers the fundamental ideas of both computer science and information technology and demonstrates how these ideas may be used to solve issues in the real world. The abilities needed by both IT specialists and computer professionals are developed in this program. The B.Sc. CSIT program provides the students with adequate theoretical and practical knowledge which will enable students to effectively participate in solving the complex problem of the IT industry.
